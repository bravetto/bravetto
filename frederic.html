ii. the fracture lines

Your science is real. Your marketing has outrun your evidence. That gap is a threat.

  • 1
    NAD ruled MacuHealth's consumer claims are not substantiated. Studies were not conducted on the current consumer product formulation. The ingredient class is validated. The specific product marketing exceeds the evidence base. BBB National Programs, September 2023
  • 2
    Memory Health's Alzheimer's claims are rejected by named independent experts. Professors Sattar, Robert Howard, and David Reynolds publicly called early trial claims "irresponsible" and "unsupported." The re-MIND RCT was underpowered: designed for 120 patients over 24 months, completed with 77 over 12. ITV News, 2018 / ISRCTN Registry / Journal of Alzheimer's Disease, 2022
  • 3
    Patents are not clinical validation. Three countries granted patents for "prevention and/or treatment of neurodegenerative disease." But patent offices evaluate novelty, not efficacy. The FDA and Alzheimer's Association explicitly warn against this conflation. FDA Consumer Warnings / Alzheimer's Association, 2019
  • 4
    Two portfolio companies have zero public evidence. Safe Balance and Natura Medica have no published clinical trials, no third-party validation, and no regulatory filings identified in exhaustive search. These are unshielded. Cross-domain analysis of 154+ public sources
  • 5
    Biomarker improvement is not disease modification. Both MacuHealth and Memory Health show strong biomarker changes (macular pigment density, blood nutrient levels). But the earlier carotenoid-only Alzheimer's RCT found no significant cognitive improvement. Biological effect does not equal clinical outcome. PubMed PMID:25408222 / re-MIND Trial Analysis
