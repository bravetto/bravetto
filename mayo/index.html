The convergence story

The work is in the middle.

OOUX and Experience Layer are already working together. The story is not a debate between them. The unfinished part is a third, middle layer — yet to be defined — that the agency has been asked to help name and bridge.

OOUX

Source truth, in place.

Canonical care objects, relationships, attributes, evidence, and ownership stay stable across surfaces. The OOUX layer is already doing its work.

Experience Layer

Decision moments, in place.

Search entry, AI answers, Care Hub pages, request paths — each is a moment where someone has to decide what to do next. The Experience Layer is already doing its work.

The gap

The middle is unnamed.

Between source truth and decision moment lives a third layer that has not yet been defined. Naming what that gap is — and how to bridge it — is the work the agency has been asked to help with.

OOUX and Experience Layer are not in competition. The convergence is the middle layer between them — the one that has not yet been named, and the part the agency is here to help define.
1. Opportunity

Serious-care patients need decision clarity, not more disconnected pages.

2. Already working

OOUX gives source truth. Experience Layer gives decision moments. Both practices are in motion.

3. The middle

The third layer between them — the practice that turns canonical objects into the experiences those moments need — is yet to be defined. That is the work.

4. What scales after

Once the middle is named and the bridge is built, the same governance can carry many surfaces and engineering can trust the pattern.
